The "Totem of Surviving" item is an early game item that can be crafted with just 8 block of iron and 1 block of emerald.

This item can only be used to save the player from death, effictively prevent them from losing their item or their hardcore world. It only actives when the player, who holds the totem in their offhand or mainhand, about to dying.

when used, the totem' usual behavior is save the player from death. Then, it summon 2 iron golem with resistance V, 5 minutes to aid them in battles. The golem will not hurt the player if they accidentally hurt it. Finally, it applies "Surviving effect" effect for 90 seconds. periodically healing the player and fills their saturation bar./p>

However, it isn't always the case. After the player has used it for 2 times. The next time, it efficient got worse. It only save the player from death if the last damage they got was below or equal 6 (3 hearts). And it only applies saturation effect for 10 seconds without the "Surviving effect". Fortunately, after the 3rd time, the totem when back normal for the 4th, 5th times before got worse again at the 6th times. The cycles continue to repeat infinitely.
